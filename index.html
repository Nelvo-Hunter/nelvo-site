The Gap

Most organizations are using AI. Almost none are seeing real results.

88% of organizations have adopted AI tools, but fewer than 1 in 10 report meaningful business impact. The gap isn't better technology. It's how the work is organized and how well the team understands what they're working with.

The difference between AI that transforms a business and AI that quietly gets abandoned comes down to two things: shared understanding across the team, and deliberate redesign of how work gets done.

AI doesn't adopt itself.

Adoption is a people challenge, not a technology challenge. When everyone on your team shares the same mental model of what AI is and what it can do for your specific business, good decisions happen naturally. When they don't, you get expensive experiments that go nowhere.
