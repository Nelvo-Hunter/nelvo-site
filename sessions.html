The sessions

Start with one session.
Stack as it pays off.

Nelvo is built in focused, two- to three-hour working sessions, booked by who is in the room and how far along they are. Each one stands alone and delivers on its own. Together, they take a team from curious to capable to compounding.

ENTRY POINT · DECISION-MAKERS
A
Strategy

Leadership Alignment

For owners, CEOs, and decision-makers. The competitive stakes, the "why now," an honest read on where your organization stands, and what safe adoption looks like. Leaders leave aligned and committed to a plan.

What's covered
  • The case for moving now, grounded in data, not hype
  • Where your organization stands, from the free diagnostic
  • A leadership playbook for adoption your team keeps
  • What safe deployment looks like in a careful business
2–3 hours · Leadership team
ENTRY POINT · FRONTLINE
B
Training

AI Fluency

For your whole team, beginners first. How AI actually works in plain language, then laptops open: everyone builds a working AI setup around their own job and gets a first real win in the room.

What's covered
  • A working mental model of AI, without the jargon
  • Context engineering: the skill that separates results from noise
  • Hands-on: build with AI on your real work, with help in the room
  • Live demonstrations of what's possible right now
2–3 hours · Everyone
C
Adoption

AI Infrastructure

For teams that have put the fluency session to work. Turns personal wins into durable systems: a memory your business owns, reusable skills, and a light rhythm of improvement. The session where it starts to compound.

What's covered
  • Owned memory: systems your business controls, not a vendor
  • Capturing what works as reusable skills
  • A personal improvement loop that doesn't need us
  • The first step toward sharing wins across the team
2–3 hours · Session B graduates
D
Adoption

Workflow Transformation

When your team is ready, we take one real workflow and redesign it end to end: deconstruct it, rebuild it around AI, prototype it live, and leave you with a pilot plan. Custom-built for your business.

What's covered
  • One workflow, chosen for real business impact
  • Redesigned around AI, not bolted onto the old process
  • Safety checks cleared before anything goes live
  • A pilot plan your team owns on day one
